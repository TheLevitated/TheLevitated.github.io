ode action

Ode Action began as Action Ode - a corridor concert of decreation - where an abandoned piano was dismantled by circular saw to the drone of a saxophone.

6 months past before we could regroup and lift out of the fragments new forms of escapism through fragile assemblage and the sounding out of space. Power tools have been replaced by solidifed forms of oxygen as together attempts are made to chanel the spirit of Hélène Smith on her voyages to Mars.

Made in collaboration with Artir Vidal, Ode Action has been performed in numerous setting from the intimacy of artist studios to annonymous high rises, obscure tunnels and bright gallery spaces.
