about // or // the practice of being


An arist and sensory researcher playing with the material world.
cwl has been educated

London College of Fashion, The Arts Student League, Central Saint Marins, University College London, and by chance meetings with those pursuing various forms of transformation; ex-cons, shamans, dancers, artists, archeologists, alchemists...


Intuitively following a path of learning-by-doing in order to share process and the fantasy narratives that drift through forms.
Work develops into temporary installations of participarory performance scenarios.
Tactile, haptic, and elemental, work is intimate, absurd, fragile, hopeful, romantic, dire.
At times following self imposed instructions;

make metal - dig the basement for myths of buried motorcycles - shread all your clothes to weave a new body - walk a wheelbarrow to the waterfall - play a broken piano for the cow...

cwl is commited to her actions.
