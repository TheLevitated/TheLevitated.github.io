CWL works with performance, sculpture sound, image, text, space, and scenarios. Often processed based, her work can be seen as a series of duets with the material world, blurring distinctions between the real and mythic with a layering of psuedo-personal-scientific-narratives. Her solitary research, digging basements for charcoal, unfolding cutting folding and melting down roofing lead into 23 ingots, burning designer goods in the volcanic desert, is often (almost) undocumented performance, relics, snapshots and text remains. CWL has compossed soundscape for theatre, radio and gallery installations. Beyound institutional education time spent time with metallurgists, archeologist, activits, martial artists, magician and shamans.
