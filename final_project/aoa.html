about // or // the practice of being


Currently living and working in London, cwl is an artist and sensory researcher playing with the material world.
Despite rumours to the contrary cwl has been educated

institutionally via London College of Fashion, The Arts Student League, Central Saint Martins, University College London, and graciously by chance meetings with those pursuing various forms of transformation; ex-cons, shamans, dancers, artists, archaeologists, alchemists...


Intuitively following a path of learning-by-(un)doing in order to share process and the fantasy narratives that drift through form, cwl's practice can be seen as a series of duets with her material through which work develops into temporary installations of participatory performance scenarios.
Tactile, haptic, and elemental, the impulse reaches towards the intimate, absurd, fragile, hopeful, romantic, dire
Following self-imposed performance instructions;

make metal - dig for myths of buried motorcycles - shred all your clothes to weave a new body - walk a wheelbarrow to the waterfall - play a broken piano for the cows...

cwl is committed to her actions.
