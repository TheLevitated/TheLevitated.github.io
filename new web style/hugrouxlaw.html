With mouths full of blood, honey, bees;
we blow kisses, and bang bang arms.

Three female artists & drummers (Hug Roux Law) play flower child hunters in an installed botanical scenography, presenting the aesthetic punk of the anthropocene as au-natural and obscure. Performances feature plants, drums, saws, diy sythnesised instruments, costume, and spoken word.
