about // or // abandoning

Currently living and working in London, cwl is an artist and sensory researcher playing with the material world.
Following process in order to share the fantasy narratives that drift through form, work incorporates sculpture, sound, image, and text, to develop into temporary installations of participarory performance scenarios.
Her sensory research, such as the unfolding, cutting, re-folding, and melting of a roof into 23 ingots, or burning designer goods in the volcanic desert, is often un-witnessed and only the relics remain to infuse and inform further actions.
Beyond an institutional education; via London College of Fashion, The Arts Student League, Central Saint Martins, University College London, time spent with those pursuing various forms of transformation; metallurgists, archaeologists, activists, martial artists, magicians, ex-cons, dancers and shamans, informs a practice that is dedicated to sensory knowledge, aka the pleasure of seeking.
